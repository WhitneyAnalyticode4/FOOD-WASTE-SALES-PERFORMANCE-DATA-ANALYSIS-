# FOOD-WASTE-AND-SALES-PERFORMANCE-DATA-ANALYSIS
A Power BI dashboard that analyzes sales, profit, and waste data to provide clear insights into product performance and business trends.

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales data to uncover insights into product performance, profitability, and overall business trends. The dashboard was built to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions by providing a clear and interactive view of key metrics.

---

##  Objectives
- Track overall sales performance
- Analyze profit and cost across different products
- Identify high-performing and underperforming items
- Monitor waste and inventory trends
- Provide actionable insights for business improvement

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- **Microsoft Excel** – Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation  

---

## Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Removed inconsistencies and unwanted symbols from raw data  
- Split merged columns into structured fields  
- Created calculated columns:
  - Total Cost  
  - Profit  
- Ensured correct data types for analysis  

---

## Dashboard Features
- **KPIs Cards**
  - Total Sales  
  - Total Profit  
  - Total Cost  
  - Waste Count  

- **Visualizations**
  - Sales by Product  
  - Profit by Product  
  - Waste Analysis  
  - Trend Analysis over time  

- **Filters/Slicers**
  - Product categories  
  - Time periods  

---

## Key Insights
- Identified top-selling and most profitable products  
- Detected products with high waste levels  
- Observed patterns in sales trends over time  
- Highlighted areas where cost reduction is needed  

---

## Impact
- Improved visibility into business performance  
- Enabled better inventory and sales decisions  
- Helped identify opportunities to increase profitability  
- Provided a foundation for reducing waste and losses
